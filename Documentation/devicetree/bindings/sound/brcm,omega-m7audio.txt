Broadcom Omega M7 Audio
- ASoC driver for interfacing to Omega's internal AudioH codec via the M7

Required properties:
	- compatible : "brcm,omega-m7audio"
	- mboxes : A mailbox is need for communicating to the M7

Example:
	omega_m7_audio: m7_audio@0 {
		compatible = "brcm,omega-m7audio";
		mboxes = <&mailbox 2>;
		status = "okay";
	};
