AK4458 115dB 768kHz 32-bit 8ch Premium DAC

Required properties:

  - compatible : "asahi-kasei,ak4458"

  - reg : the I2C address of the device

Optional properties:

  - amp-mute-gpio : gpio used to mute external analog amplifier

Example:

ak4458_codec: ak4458@10 {
	compatible = "asahi-kasei,ak4458";
	reg = <0x10>;
	amp-mute-gpio = <&gpio_asiu 43 0>;
};
