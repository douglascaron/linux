* Broadcom iProc Digital Timing Engine(DTE) based PTP clock driver

Required properties:
- compatible: should be "brcm,ptp-iproc-dte"
- reg: address and length of the DTE block's NCO registers

Example:

ptp_dte: ptp_dte@180af650 {
	compatible = "brcm,ptp-iproc-dte";
	reg = <0x180af650 0x10>;
	status = "okay";
};
