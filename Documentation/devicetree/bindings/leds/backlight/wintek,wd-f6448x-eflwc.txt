Wintek LCD Panel bindings
-----------------------------------------------

The Wintek LCD panel driver toggles GPIO to power and reset LCD panel,
then sends SPI sequences to initialize the panel.

Required properties:

	- compatible:        "wintek,wd-f6448x-eflwc"
	- reg:               SPI chip select number for the device
	- spi-max-frequency: Max SPI frequency to use
	- power-enable-gpio: GPIO to enable LCD panel power
	- reset-gpio:        GPIO to reset LCD panel

Example:

	panel@0 {
		compatible = "wintek,wd-f6448x-eflwc";
		spi-max-frequency = <500000>;
		reg = <0>;
		power-enable-gpio = <&gpio_asiu 64 0>;
		reset-gpio = <&gpio_asiu 68 0>;
	};
